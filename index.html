Research:

My research intrest lies in developing consistent numerical schemes for the equations of geophysical fluid dynamics (mainly the shallow water equations). Here, I focus on the framework of variational discretization. Further, I have a growing interest in stochastic fluid dynamics.

Publications:

  • R. Brecht, A. Bihlo, S. MacLachlan and J. Behrens, 2018. A well-balanced meshless tsunami propagation and inundation model. Adv. Water Resour., 115, 273–285, arXiv:1705.09831 .
